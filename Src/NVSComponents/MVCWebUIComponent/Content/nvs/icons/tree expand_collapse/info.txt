Tree, Expand/Collapse Icon 
------------------------------------------

Created: 2007-06-29
By: Design & Usability, Volvo Information Technology, dept 9615


Description of usage:
Expand or collapse container contents when navigating through a hierarchy. The item stays expanded until the user collapse it. Two opposite icons. May be showed together at the same time. 
	
Action:
Expand or collapse a folder view.

Action mandatory:
Yes

Note:
Not available in all sizes. Used in tables and tree hierarchy.
